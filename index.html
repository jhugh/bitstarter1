2055 AD - the struggle between the USA and China for world domination has reached crisis point. A hopelessly divided America has become inward-looking and isolated while China has grasped control of much of the world's energy and mineral resources. Tension escalates as Taiwan becomes the focus for a possible outbreak of war. It is at this moment that Stanford professor, Jack Jackson, announces an astounding breakthrough at the leading edge of technology - an AI entity that is not only smarter than any human being but that continues to become more intelligent. In a race against time can Jack Jackson's AI become intelligent enough to help prevent war between the USA and China? Or will the AI entity itself become a bigger danger to the world than the rivalry of China and the USA?
